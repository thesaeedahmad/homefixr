# User Manual — HomeFixr
*Iteration 14. How to use the application, by role.*

HomeFixr connects homeowners ("Customers") with professionals ("Providers").
An Administrator oversees verification and fraud.

## Getting started
1. Open the site and click **Get started**.
2. Choose your role — **Hire a professional** (Customer) or **Offer my services**
   (Provider) — and register with your name, email, and a password.
3. You land on your **Dashboard**, tailored to your role.

---

## For Customers
**Post a job**
- Dashboard → **Post a job**. Choose a category, add a title, description, and
  location, optionally upload photos. A **fair-price suggestion** appears to guide
  your budget. Submit.

**Compare and accept bids**
- Open your job (Dashboard → **My jobs**). Under **Bids**, compare each provider's
  rate × hours + equipment = total, plus their **Verified** badge and rating.
- Click **Accept bid**. The job moves to *In progress* and other bids are declined.

**Pay and complete (escrow)**
- In **Escrow payment**, click **Pay into escrow** (simulated — no real money).
- When the provider marks the work done, click **Confirm & release**. The job
  becomes *Completed*. If something's wrong, use **Dispute** to refund.

**Review**
- After completion, rate your provider (1–5 stars + comment).

**Chat & notifications**
- Use the **chat** on the job page to message your provider. The bell shows
  unread **notifications** (new bids, messages, payments).

---

## For Providers
**Get verified**
- Dashboard → **Get verified**. Upload your ID and trade-license images. An admin
  reviews them; once **approved**, a trust badge appears on your bids.

**Find work and bid**
- **Browse** jobs and filter by category/location. Open a job and **Place a bid**
  (hourly rate, hours, equipment cost). The total updates live.

**Do the work and get paid**
- When your bid is accepted and the customer funds escrow, you'll be notified.
  Do the work, then **Mark work as done**. The customer releases the payment.

---

## For Administrators
- Log in with the seeded admin account. The **Admin** area has:
  - **Overview** — platform counts.
  - **Verifications** — approve/reject provider documents.
  - **Fraud** — review system-flagged bids/accounts (Confirm/Dismiss).
  - **Users** and **Jobs** — full lists.

---

## Tips
- Status is always shown as a coloured badge (e.g. *Open*, *Payment held*,
  *Verified*).
- Most actions confirm before doing anything irreversible.
- The interface is keyboard-accessible and works on mobile and desktop.
